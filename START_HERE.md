   - Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP

   - Place in: `assets/music/`
   - Name: `background.mp3`
   - Duration: 2+ minutes
   - Mood: Soft, emotional, instrumental

3. **Memory Video**
   - Place in: `assets/videos/`
   - Name: `memory.mp4`
   - Duration: 2-5 minutes

   - Count: Exactly 15 captions
